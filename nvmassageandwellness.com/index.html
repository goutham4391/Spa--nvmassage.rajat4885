About The Owner

April Rose RN, LMT is the Owner of Napa Valley Massage & Wellness. With an extensive mix of medical and holistic spa background, April keeps the collective of technicians current with the latest techniques and treatments. She schedules classes, workshops and trade sessions for her therapists to attend and grow. Her mission is to provide therapeutic treatments and impart relaxation techniques in to your daily life. Her team of holistic practitioners will work with you to achieve your wellness goals.
